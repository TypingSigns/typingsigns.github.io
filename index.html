About

The Goal of Typing Signs is to make ASL and other Sign Languages more accessible. Part of this involves easing the spread of Sign Language through printed mediums like books.

Because:

"Die Grenzen meiner Sprache bedeuten die Grenzen meiner Welt." ("The limits of my language are the limits of my world.") - Ludwig Wittgenstein
